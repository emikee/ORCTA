Who We Are

The Oregon Chinese Tennis Association (ORCTA) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization established in 2023. We are registered as a USTA community tennis association in the Pacific Northwest Section.

Our Vision

We hope to inspire ethnic and cultural inclusion through a shared passion for tennis.

Our Mission

Through tennis, we hope to promote well-being, education, and cultural understanding in the local Chinese American community while building bridges of tolerance with other ethnic groups. More specifically, we would like to...

  • Use tennis to boost well-being for local Chinese Americans.
  • Offer educational chances for young tennis players through camps and exhibition matches with both national and international pros
  • Strengthen cultural understanding in the local Chinese community with recreational tennis matches
  • Promote understanding and tolerance between Chinese and other ethnic communities through communal tennis matches
