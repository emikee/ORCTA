Meet Our Team

Dan Chen

Rotating Chairman

Dan, a dedicated tennis enthusiast, started his tennis journey in 2005 in China. After moving to the U.S. in 2018, he carried his strong love for tennis. Over the years, he improved his skills through local clubs and competitive tournaments in his region, learning important lessons about determination, hard work, and being a good sport.

Since coming to America, Dan easily became part of different tennis groups, fully enjoying the diverse tennis culture. Whether he's playing on the court or watching a match, tennis always makes Dan happy and helps him grow as a person, something he's really thankful for.

Tao Hua

Treasurer

Tao, who moved to Oregon from Massachusetts in 2005, discovered her love for tennis in 2014 while waiting for her kids' activities. Her journey led to competing in USTA leagues, achieving Sectionals and Nationals. As a captain for women's and mixed teams since 2019, she values inclusivity and community.

Tao's leadership, organizational skills, and passion drive her role in the Oregon Chinese Tennis Association (ORCTA), focusing on unity, well-being, and community impact. Beyond tennis, she enjoys painting, gardening, traveling, cooking, and time with friends.
